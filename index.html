Courtesy visit to the DG/CEO Brg. Gen. MUHAMMAD BUBA MARWA at the NDLEA headquarters Abuja

The meeting laid the foundation for long-term collaboration between GAPEEWY and the NDLEA. The NDLEA chair gave the organization important insights on how to contribute to solving the issue of drug abuse in the community.

Visit to Kaduna State Command of the NDLEA

In continuation of efforts to get rid of some prevailing social menace which included drug abuse, the leadership of GAPEEWY was in the Kaduna State Command of the NDLEA. Strategic issues where discussed and the State Commander pledges his support towards GAPEEWY's drive in achieving the same feat.
