Our Vision

At GAPEEWY, our vision is a world where every woman and youth, regardless of their circumstances, has the opportunity to thrive, shape their destiny, and contribute to building peaceful, prosperous, and resilient communities. We envision a future where gender equality, social justice, and economic empowerment are not just ideals but everyday realities.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: Empowering individuals to take control of their lives and reach their full potential is fundamental to GAPEEWY's mission.
  • Equality: Promoting equal access to opportunities and resources is essential for building a fair and inclusive society.
  • Integrity: Upholding integrity ensures that GAPEEWY operates transparently and maintains trust with beneficiaries, partners, and stakeholders.
  • Commitment: Demonstrating a strong commitment to the mission and those served by GAPEEWY is vital for sustained impact.
  • Peace: Advocating for peaceful solutions to conflicts and promoting harmony within communities aligns with the organization's goal of social betterment.
  • Economic Empowerment: Providing the means for financial independence and sustainable livelihoods helps individuals break free from cycles of poverty.
  • Social Justice: Working towards a more just and inclusive world supports GAPEEWY's mission of empowering marginalized populations.
  • Self-Determination: Encouraging individuals to make choices that align with their aspirations is important for personal growth and empowerment.
  • Community Engagement: Collaborating with local stakeholders fosters lasting change within communities.
  • Resilience: Helping individuals develop resilience is crucial for overcoming adversity and building self-confidence.
  • Education: Recognizing the power of education and ensuring access to quality learning opportunities is essential for breaking the cycle of ignorance and poverty.
  • Innovation: Embracing innovation allows GAPEEWY to adapt and address evolving community needs.
  • Honesty: Maintaining honesty in communication and actions is vital for building trust within the organization and with external partners.
  • Confidence: Instilling confidence in individuals empowers them to believe in their abilities and potential to effect change.
